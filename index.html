Features

Everything a nutrition app
for athletes should have

Built by an exercise scientist who got frustrated with generic calorie trackers that ignore training load.

📈
Periodised targets

Carb and calorie targets scale with session RPE and duration. Set your own upper/lower limits per hour and control exactly how targets ramp up with intensity.

🔄
Intervals.icu sync

Pulls in your planned and completed sessions automatically. Sets pre/post and during-session fuel targets separately for each workout. Exports nutrition data back as wellness fields.

🍽️
Saved meal templates

Save food combinations as named templates and reload them with one click. No re-entering the same breakfast every morning when you eat similar things on similar training days.

🧮
Suggest quantities

Select your foods for a meal and it calculates how much of each to eat to best hit your remaining carb and calorie targets. Useful when you're wrecked after a long session.

📊
Trend tracking

See how your actual intake compares to targets over time. Daily totals sync back to Intervals.icu wellness fields so nutrition data sits alongside your training load.

🥗
USDA food database

Search thousands of foods from the USDA FoodData Central database, or add your own custom foods with full macro and serving size control.

How it works

Set up in minutes

Connect your training data, set your targets, and start fuelling with precision.

01
Set your baseline targets

Enter your bodyweight and baseline macro targets (carbs/kg, protein/kg, fat/kg). These form the foundation of your daily nutrition on rest days.

02
Configure your intensity scaling

Set the upper and lower carb and calorie limits per hour, the RPE point where targets start ramping up, and how steeply they increase. Fully customisable to your training style.

03
Sync your sessions

Connect Intervals.icu to automatically pull in your training sessions. Each session gets its own pre/post and during-session fuel targets based on duration and intensity.

04
Log food and track trends

Log meals using saved templates or the USDA database. Use Suggest quantities to hit your targets. Save daily totals and watch trends build over weeks and months.
